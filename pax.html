Pax A60

HYBRID Android MiniPOS

Weighing only 300g, the A60 offers all the advantages of the A-series payment terminals in a featherweight, elegant design. It is paperless, helping reduce your environmental footprint as well as stationery costs. Chose a version with the fingerprint reader if you need to process higher valued transactions. With the BP60A docking station, you can pair up to seven A60 terminals simultaneously for printing, charging and additional peripherals connectivity.
