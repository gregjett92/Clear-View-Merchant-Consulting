About Us

A Clear Goal

Headquartered on the outskirts of San Antonio, Texas, ClearView Merchant Consulting was founded by payment professionals who aspired to build unique and lasting relationships with their clients, employees, and partners, by truly seeing the needs of, and caring for the individual business. Finding it bothersome to see small businesses being taken advantage of by big corporations, this concept formed. We are happy to see our vision a reality today. From our business' conception we have provided innovative and specialized solutions to our clients. These solutions have helped them reduce processing costs, streamline operations, and increase revenue through the use of additional payment options.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to cultivate life-long relationships with our customers, employees, and partners by delivering extraordinary service and value every day.  The growth of your business is our purpose.
