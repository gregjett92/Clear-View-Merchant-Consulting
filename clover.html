HOTSAUCE

HotSauce Technologies has been the leading software provider for hospitality applications serving restaurants, bars and nightclubs since 1998. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, the company has grown tremendously due to the incredible demands for a powerful, reliable, yet cost effective product. By utilizing the latest technology and stellar support, HotSauce Technologies has become the leading provider of POS systems in the industry today.

Service, Service, Service

HotSauce are committed to making you their number one priority with their 24/7 customer support. From initial contact, the team will work with you to configure your system according to the specific needs of your business including building menus, management training, stuff training, installation and 8 hours go live services

Business Intelligence Reporting

With HotSauce™ Business Intelligence you can effectively capture, organize and analyze your business information. The flexibility of the reporting gives you the ability to generate a variety of reports such as Sales Reports, Audit Trail Reports and Employee reports, to help you make fast and informed business decisions.

24/7 Live Support

HotSauce™ Technologies pioneered 24/7 LIVE support. Their professional team is committed to providing you superior support for software, hardware and training needs

Real Time Data Redundancy

With HotSauce™ Real Time Data Redundancy your business will always run smoothly without loss of information.  Using SQL Server Technology, HotSauce™ provides continuous real time data replication by storing a copy of your database into a separate computer.

Back Office

HotSauce™ Back Office is a simple, powerful tool that allows you to configure the Front of the House system to fit your business operations. Your POS system can be easily modified to accommodate your changing business needs.

TAKE CHARGE

Take Charge Anywhere is a mobile processing platform for iOS and Android devices that utilizes the popular Cash Discount checkout process. Take Charge will virtually eliminate merchant’s processing fees, while providing their customers with more options to pay.

Take Charge Anywhere runs on any iOS or Android device. You’ll simply install the application onto your device, pair the Bluetooth or audio jack card reader, and you’re ready to start processing!

Each merchant boarded on Take Charge Anywhere will receive access to their own Take Charge Anywhere Merchant Portal. Manage users, track records of previous batches, and manage app settings from any desktop or mobile device.
